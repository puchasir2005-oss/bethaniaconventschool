Section 01

Introduction

1.1 Purpose

This Software Requirements Specification (SRS) document provides a comprehensive description of the Bethania Convent School official website — its features, technical architecture, functional and non-functional requirements, and design guidelines. It serves as the authoritative reference for the client to review, approve, and track all current and planned features.

1.2 Scope

The website is a multi-page informational web application designed to:

  • Present the school's identity, values, history, and motto to prospective parents and students
  • Showcase the campus, faculty, academic programs, sports, and co-curricular activities
  • Provide contact information and admission enquiry channels
  • Integrate social media presence (Instagram feed)
  • Deliver a premium, modern, and visually engaging user experience

Section 05

Page-by-Page Feature Specification

5.1 Home Page (/)

Page Flow:

Hero → Motto → Gallery → Instagram → Faculty → Sports → CTA → Footer

Hero Section

  • Full-bleed background image (hero-desk.png) with overlay gradient
  • H1 headline with italic emphasis · Descriptive subtitle · Two CTA buttons · Statistics bar (4 items)

Campus Gallery & Instagram

  • 5-item mosaic grid with hover overlays, captions, and parallax scrolling
  • Instagram section: 5-item grid with camera emoji hover overlay

Faculty & Sports

  • 4 faculty region cards: Punjab, Kerala, Pune, Darjeeling
  • Split layout with 8 sports: Table Tennis, Basketball, Badminton, Cricket, Volleyball, Kho-Kho, Skating, Karate

5.2 About Page (/about)

  • Page header with cream background · Long-form prose (8 paragraphs)
  • Topics: History, Congregation, Administration, Patron Saint, Motto, Service, Campus, Tradition

5.3 Vision & Mission Page (/vision)

  • Three subsections: Motto ("Lighted for Life"), Mission, Vision
  • Vision includes 3 bulleted goals · Closing statement centered in bold

5.4 Programs Page (/programs)

7 numbered feature sections:

  1. Diverse, Nationally Sourced Faculty
  2. Advanced Language & Communication Hub
  3. Future-Ready Digital Infrastructure (AI Lab, Computer Lab)
  4. Culture of Reading (Library)
  5. Creative & Martial Arts (Skating, Karate, Music, Dance)
  6. Dynamic Sports & Athletic Programs
  7. Celebrating Culture & Global Awareness
